Warp SMP Plugin Overview

Gaining Points: Earn +1 Time Point for each player kill.

Activation: Use shift + offhand binds to activate Time Anomaly abilities.

Cooldown: All abilities have a 3-minute cooldown.

Time Anomaly Abilities:

  • +1 Point: Time Blink – Freeze players in a 3-block radius for 2 seconds.
  • +2 Points: Time Shift – Freeze players in a 3-block radius for 3 seconds.
  • +3 Points: Time Bubble – Freeze players in a 4-block radius for 4 seconds.
  • +4 Points: Time Burst – Freeze players in a 5-block radius for 5 seconds.
  • +5 Points: Time Mastery – Freeze players in a 6-block radius for 6 seconds.

Losing Time Points:

Each death deducts 1 Time Point. Falling into negative points results in penalties:

  • -1 Point: 10% speed reduction
  • -2 Points: 20% speed reduction
  • -3 Points: 30% speed reduction
  • -4 Points: 40% speed reduction
  • -5 Points: 50% speed reduction
  • -6 Points: Permanent ban from the SMP

Starting Items:

Upon joining, you receive one of these breaks to counter freeze effects:

  • Chrono Shield: +8 hearts & Resistance II for 10 sec (Cooldown: 4 min)
  • Eternal Strike: +2 Attack Damage & +50% Attack Speed for 8 sec (Cooldown: 4 min)
  • Momentum Shift: Speed IV & Jump Boost III for 15 sec (Cooldown: 4 min)
  • Temporal Surge: Strength II & Haste II for 15 sec (Cooldown: 4 min)
